Coffee Supply Chain Innovation

A larger-scale goal of Gratified is to improve trade route efficiencies, allowing more direct and beneficial purchasing from East African coffee producers. This requires scale, relationships, and expertise that we'll build over time.

Near-term: Partner with roasters who prioritize fair trade and direct relationships. Feature their sourcing stories. Build our understanding of the supply chain.

Long-term: As we grow to multiple locations, work directly with importers and eventually producers to create more efficient, transparent, and beneficial supply chains. Use our platform to set new standards for ethical sourcing.
